           Recent Progress in the Friend 21 Project
                    <speaker>Dr. Hajime Nonogaki</speaker>
<paragraph>                      Fujitsu Limited</paragraph>

<paragraph>                Monday May 11, <<stime>10:30 am</stime> - <etime>11:00 am</etime>></paragraph>

                         <<location>Wean Hall 8220</location>>
<paragraph>      The Friend21 Project is a MITI sponsored effort to</paragraph>

      develop a comfortable personal information system
      for the 21st century.  A major concern is the user
      interface, and one approach under current study is
      the development of rich interface agents.  This talk
      will discuss the user interface for Friend21 and
      present recent progress toward the project's goals.
